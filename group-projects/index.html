>Group Projects WebPages of LCG (Laboratorio di Computer Grafica) C2 2024-2025, Bachelor Communication Design

Web pages for each group of the course listed by their ID. Students decided a theme to represent with this quantiative and qualitative visualizations, searching for dataset(s) and defining how to process it.

  • G1) Geografia delle Risorse - Resource Geography
  • G2) Rifiuti Spaziali - Space trash
  • G3) A Thousand Splendid Suns
  • G4) Ti fidi degli altri? - Do you trust the others?
  • G5) Censura Film - Movies censorship
  • G6) Il sovraffollamento delle carceri in Italia - Italian's Prisons overcrowding
  • G7) Costo e accessibilità ad una dieta sana - Healthy Diet costs and accessibility
  • G8) Flowers that shouldn't bloom
  • G9) La visualizzazione dei vangeli - The gospel visualization
